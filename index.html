Anxiety

Defintion: A feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease, typically about an imminent event or something with an uncertain outcome.

Breathe. Inhale like you're sipping through a straw. Exhale through your teeth making a "tssss" sound. Gain control over your anxiety. You can do it.

Depression

Definition: Feelings of severe despondency and dejection.

Get in a routine and set goals - but when you need a break, you need a break. Catch your breath and sit. You are stronger than you think. Much stronger. You're doing well.
